Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2025

Introduction

Welcome to RoamQuest ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our RoamQuest mobile application (the "App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not use our App.

Information We Collect

1. Information You Provide to Us

Account Information

  • Email Address: When you create an account, we collect your email address for authentication and communication purposes.
  • Apple Sign-In: If you choose to sign in with Apple, we receive your email address and name from Apple.

User-Generated Content

  • Photos: When you check in to locations, you may choose to take photos or select photos from your library. These photos are stored on our servers.
  • Location Data: When you use check-in features, we may collect your current latitude and longitude coordinates.

Travel Data

  • Cities You Visit: We track which cities you've explored to provide personalized recommendations.
  • Check-in History: We maintain a record of places you've visited and checked off your list.

2. Information Automatically Collected

Location Information

  • Current Location: We collect your precise location to detect your current city and generate personalized checklists.
  • Background Location: The app may request location access when in use to provide city detection functionality.

Device Information

  • Device Identifier: A unique identifier for your mobile device.
  • Device Type: Model and operating system version.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the App, features used, and performance data.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase (Backend Services) — Stores user accounts, checklists, and check-in data. Hosts user photos.
  • Apple (Sign In & In-App Purchase) — Provides authentication services and processes payments.

How We Use Your Information

Purpose Description
Account Management To create and manage your account, authenticate you, and provide customer support.
Core Functionality To detect your current city and generate personalized travel checklists.
Check-in Features To enable photo check-ins, track your visited places, and create travel reports.
Service Improvement To analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, and improve the App's functionality.
Communication To send you important updates about the App (with your consent).
Payment Processing To process in-app purchases for premium subscriptions.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With third-party companies who perform services on our behalf (e.g., Supabase for hosting). These service providers have access to your information only to perform these services.
  • Legal Requirements: To comply with legal obligations, court orders, or government requests; to protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property; to prevent fraud or other illegal activities.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with any merger, sale of company assets, or acquisition of all or a portion of our business.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS.
  • Secure Storage: User data is stored securely with access controls.
  • Authentication: Password protection and optional biometric authentication.
  • Regular Audits: We regularly review our security practices.

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us or using the in-app deletion feature.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten): Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured format.
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your data.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time (where processing is based on consent).

Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
  • Sending you an in-app notification (for significant changes).

The updated policy will be effective when posted.
